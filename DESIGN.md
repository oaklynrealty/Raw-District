# Raw District Ads Campaign Design System

## Direction

Conversion-first editorial property showcase for paid Google Ads and social traffic.

## Tokens

- Background: warm white `#f8f5ef`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Text: deep navy `#102838`
- Muted text: `#61717a`
- Border: `#ded8ce`
- Primary CTA: copper `#a6421b`
- CTA hover: `#7b2f12`
- Radius: `8px`
- Spacing: 8px scale
- Typeface: system sans-serif stack

## Components

- Minimal header with logo and advisor call link.
- Two-column desktop hero and compact mobile hero.
- Multi-step lead form card with progress, selectable cards, inline validation and thank-you state.
- Variant B hero with modal or mobile bottom-sheet form.
- Compact trust strip.
- Benefit cards, gallery grid, static map preview, advisor card, FAQ accordion and final conversion band.

## Rules

- One primary CTA phrase: "Show Me Prices & Availability".
- No fake awards, ratings, logos, scarcity or guaranteed investment claims.
- Placeholder trust/advisor/testimonial content remains visibly labelled until verified.
